Vishakha Kapoor

FYP Undergraduate Student

Vishakha is now a Research Scientist in the Department of Materials Science and Engineering National University of Singapore.

I’m a Year 4 undergraduate student in Materials Science and Engineering at the National University of Singapore. I’m passionate about sustainability, and I’m particularly interested in solar energy and battery materials. I’m excited to learn about the application of computational techniques to the study and development of sustainable energy technologies. In my free time, I love to read, do crossword puzzles, explore new sudoku variants, and play survival and sandbox games.
