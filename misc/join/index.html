We are currently hiring new group members

Our lab is hiring motivated Graduate students and postdoctoral Researchers. This page is regularly updated depending on the availability of research fund.

Our interest sits at the interface between computational materials science and the development of new materials for applications in energy storage and conversion. We are looking for industrious and inquisitive candidates that can bring novel and interdisciplinary momentum to the field of energy storage. We are committed to improving diversity in materials sciences. Applicants from diverse backgrounds are encouraged to apply.
